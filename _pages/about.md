I am a first-year Ph.D student in the Electrical & Computer Engineering department at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. My area of research is in power electronics, particularly in matrix converter topologies applied to a variety of applications such as isolated motor drives, switched power amplifiers and single-wire earth return power transmission.
